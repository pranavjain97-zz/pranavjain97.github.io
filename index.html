About Me

I've been designing professionally for over several years - working at a Search Marketing firm, working for design agencies, and taking on my own clients and projects. Now I am a full-time freelancer, with a skills list outside of the regular “web designer” title.

Although I have a BA in Visual Communications, I continually strive to learn as much as I can and grow as designer/developer.

Over the past years, I have worked on many different types of projects - from web applications, responsive websites, front-end development, company branding, Wordpress Development, and everything in between. By combining my diverse disciplines, I provide services for businesses all shapes and sizes. My goal is to keep my design work simple and coding clean and functional, no matter how complex the project may be.
